# NutriAware — Professional Research Questionnaire (English Version v3.0)

> **Study Title**: Food Safety and Child Nutrition Intervention  
> **Platform**: NutriAware — Malnutrition Intelligence Platform  
> **Target Population**: Parents/guardians of children aged 3–14 years  
> **Design**: Quasi-experimental (Pre-test / Post-test with control)  
> **Frameworks**: KAP Model · FAO-DDS · WHO Anthropometry · TPB · TAM  
> **Estimated Completion Time**: 10–12 minutes

## Section 8: FAO Dietary Diversity Score (DDS) — 24-Hour Recall

*Construct: FAO Minimum Dietary Diversity Indicator · Response: Yes (1) / No (0)*

| # | ID | Food Group | Examples | Yes | No |
|---|-----|------------|----------|-----|-----|
| 8.1 | DDS1 | Cereals and grains | Rice, pasta, bread, oats, corn | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.2 | DDS2 | Legumes | Beans, lentils, chickpeas, peas | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.3 | DDS3 | Milk and dairy products | Milk, yogurt, cheese, labneh | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.4 | DDS4 | Meat, poultry, or fish | Beef, chicken, fish, liver | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.5 | DDS5 | Eggs | Chicken eggs, quail eggs | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.6 | DDS6 | Dark green leafy vegetables | Spinach, kale, lettuce, parsley | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.7 | DDS7 | Vitamin A–rich fruits/vegetables | Carrot, mango, sweet potato, pumpkin | ☐ | ☐ |
| 8.8 | DDS8 | Other fruits or vegetables | Apple, banana, tomato, cucumber | ☐ | ☐ |

> **Scoring Rule**:  
> DDS = Sum of "Yes" responses (range 0–8)  
> **Adequate dietary diversity** = DDS ≥ 5 food groups  
> Each item is coded as binary: Yes = 1, No = 0

## Anthropometric Assessment Form (Researcher-Completed Only)

> ⚠️ **This form is NOT part of the participant survey.** It is completed by trained research staff only.

| # | Variable ID | Measurement | Instrument | Instructions |
|---|-------------|-------------|------------|--------------|
| A.1 | ANTH_WEIGHT | Child weight (kg) | Digital scale (0.1 kg precision) | Child without shoes, light clothing |
| A.2 | ANTH_HEIGHT | Child height/length (cm) | Stadiometer (0.1 cm precision) | Frankfurt plane position |
| A.3 | ANTH_DOB | Date of birth | Records / parent report | dd/mm/yyyy |
| A.4 | ANTH_AGE | Age in months | Auto-calculated | (Measurement date − DOB) |
| A.5 | ANTH_SEX | Sex | Records | Male = 1 · Female = 2 |
| A.6 | ANTH_DATE | Measurement date | — | dd/mm/yyyy |

> **Note**: Z-scores will be calculated using WHO AnthroPlus standards:
> - **WAZ** (Weight-for-Age Z-score)
> - **HAZ** (Height-for-Age Z-score)
> - **BAZ** (BMI-for-Age Z-score)
>
> Classification: Z < −3 = Severe · −3 ≤ Z < −2 = Moderate · −2 ≤ Z ≤ 2 = Normal · Z > 2 = Overweight/Obese
